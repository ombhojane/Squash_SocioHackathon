Well-being Schemes and Seminars for Women

Maternity Support Scheme

This scheme provides assistance and support to pregnant women, offering resources for healthcare, nutrition, and maternity leave options.

Duration: 2 Years

Career Re-entry Program

Designed for women who are returning to the workforce post-pregnancy, this program offers training, mentorship, and flexible work options.

Duration: 2 Years

Child Care Assistance Program

Aiding working mothers with child care solutions, this program provides financial support and access to quality child care services.

Duration: 2 Years
